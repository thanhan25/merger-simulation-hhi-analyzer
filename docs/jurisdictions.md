
# Global Antitrust Jurisdictions

While `merger-sim` defaults to the U.S. DOJ/FTC 2023 Guidelines, HHI thresholds vary globally.

## European Commission (EC)

The EC relies on HHI but enforces slightly different "Safe Harbor" thresholds under the EU Merger Regulation (EUMR):

* **Safe Harbor 1:** Post-merger HHI < 1000.
* **Safe Harbor 2:** Post-merger HHI is between 1000 and 2000, and the $\Delta$ HHI < 250.
* **Safe Harbor 3:** Post-merger HHI > 2000, but the $\Delta$ HHI < 150.

## Germany (Bundeskartellamt)

The German Federal Cartel Office traditionally scrutinizes mergers creating or strengthening a dominant position. While HHI is used as a screening tool, statutory presumptions of dominance (e.g., a single firm reaching a 40% market share) often override strict HHI calculations.
